STAR WARS themed 3rd GRADE LESSON PLAN

How cool would it be to teach 3rd graders physical science through a series of activities designed from the ground up to both be educational and fun at the same time.

Let's Start



The five Stations

The story goes where the FORCE is stranded on a planet after their ship crashed.

  • Scavenging for ship pieces
  • Making the necessary tools
  • Blowing up the death star
  • Building defensive weapons
  • Galactic Battles!!

Scavenging for Ship Pieces

Rizwaan Abdul

When our ship crashed, ship pieces went flying in every direction. I developed a method to seperate the metal pieces using the physical properties of magnetism, size, shape, and color.

Making the tools

Zora McKissic

Students will learn how to make slime!

Blowing up the death star

Deacon Murphy

Students will explore physical properties by popping a balloon.

Building defensive weapons

Garrett Behlau

We make shields as protection for the passengers from our ship.

Galactic Battles!!!

A group activity

Galactic battles is review game to test student's knowledge gained through these activities.
